Tasty Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe is the perfected lasagna recipe. It is extremely easy to make and tasty.

All of my friends love this recipe and it is a hit at any event I bring it to. You will enjoy it as well.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Velveeta Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef and drain
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Layer the pasta, sauce, ground beef, and cheese
  4. Put in the oven at 350 for 20 minutes
  5. Top with parmesean cheese at the end
