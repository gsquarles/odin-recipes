Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Description

My homeade chicken alfredo recipe. Quick, easy and delicious.

All of my friends love this recipe and it is a hit at any event I bring it to. You will enjoy it as well.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Bring Water to a boil
  2. Season Chicken Breast however you want and cook how you like
  3. Melt butter in pot on low
  4. Once butter is melted pour in one cup heavy cream
  5. Let heavy cream simmer into a low boil
  6. Once boiling slowly mix in parmesean cheese while stirring
  7. Continue to add cheese until consistency you like
  8. Mix everything together and garnish with parsley
