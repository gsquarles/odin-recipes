Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

Easy Homeade mashed potatoes recipe.

Made in less than 30 minutes. Easy for a quick dinner side.

Ingredients

  • Golden Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring Water to a boil and cook potatoes until fork tender
  2. After put potatoes in bowl and mash
  3. After a mashed consistency mix in milk and butter
  4. Continue to mash and mix until nice consistency
  5. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
